Healthcare

Patients deserve care,
not long waits.

In hospitals and clinics, long OPD queues create anxiety, crowd corridors, and overload reception staff. GoStationery's Kiosk transforms the patient journey — from chaotic waiting rooms to calm, organised service.

  • ✅Reduce OPD corridor crowding with digital tokens and SMS alerts
  • ✅Separate queues per department: General, Specialist, Lab, Pharmacy
  • ✅Integrate with HIS / appointment booking systems via API
  • ✅Print prescription slips and lab forms from the Print Station
  • ✅Real-time occupancy alerts for administration

Branch Queue — Live
Banking & Finance

Fair, fast service for every customer.

Bank branches, NBFCs, and insurance offices handle high footfall daily. A chaotic branch hurts NPS scores and branch reputation. Kiosk delivers fair, transparent queuing that customers trust.

  • ✅Separate queues for tellers, loans, KYC, lockers, and priority customers
  • ✅SMS alerts so customers wait outside — not standing in line
  • ✅Branch manager dashboard with real-time wait-time and staff metrics
  • ✅Customer satisfaction ratings after every interaction
  • ✅Print forms, account statements, and receipts from the Print Station

Counter Queue — Live
Retail

Let shoppers browse,
not wait in line.

Retail counters — billing, customer service, returns, tailoring — often become bottlenecks that frustrate shoppers and reduce dwell time. Virtual queuing keeps customers in the store and in a good mood.

  • ✅Virtual tokens let customers shop while waiting for their turn
  • ✅Display boards at counters show live queue status
  • ✅Peak-hour analytics to plan staffing in advance
  • ✅Print Station for price tags, invoices, and gift wrapping forms
  • ✅Customer satisfaction scores per counter
