Our Story

From a frustrating wait to a nationwide solution

GoStationery was born in a hospital waiting room. Our founder spent three hours waiting for an OPD token — watching nurses manually manage a crowd of frustrated patients with no system, no structure, and no visibility.

That frustration became a mission. We built GoStationery to bring world-class queue management and smart stationery dispensing to every business in India — not just large enterprises, but clinics, banks, colleges, and government offices too.

2021

Company Founded

GoStationery incorporated in Ahmedabad with a seed team of 4 engineers and 1 business co-founder.

2022

Kiosk v1 Launched

First queue management kiosk deployed at a multispeciality hospital in Gujarat. Wait times dropped 40% in month one.

2023

Print Station Launched

Introduced the Print & Dispense Station after corporate clients requested a self-service stationery solution.

2024

100+ Clients, 6 Industries

Expanded to banking, retail, government, and education verticals. Crossed 100 client milestone and 500+ locations.

2025

AI & Cloud Platform

Launched AI-powered wait prediction and unified cloud dashboard. Pan-India service coverage established.
