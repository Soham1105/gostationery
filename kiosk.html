Features

Everything you need to manage queues perfectly

From token generation to analytics, Kiosk covers the entire visitor journey — all in one intelligent platform.

🎫

Digital Token Issuance

Visitors get tokens via the kiosk touchscreen, QR code scan, SMS link, or WhatsApp — no physical tickets, no crowding.

📺

Live Display Boards

Wall-mounted or TV displays show current serving numbers, estimated wait times, and counter assignments — updated in real time.

🧠

AI Wait-Time Prediction

Machine learning analyses historical patterns to predict wait times with high accuracy — giving visitors reliable estimates at token generation.

🏢

Multi-Counter & Multi-Branch

Manage multiple service counters, departments, and even multiple locations from a single cloud dashboard with unified reporting.

📲

SMS & WhatsApp Alerts

Visitors receive automated notifications when their turn is near — so they can wait anywhere, not just in the queue.

⭐

Customer Feedback

Collect satisfaction ratings after every service interaction via the kiosk or SMS survey — understand staff performance and service quality.

📊

Advanced Analytics

Peak hour heatmaps, average service time, counter utilisation, abandonment rate, and staff KPIs — all exportable to Excel or PDF.

🔗

API Integration

REST APIs connect Kiosk with your HIS, CRM, ERP, or appointment system — creating a fully unified patient or customer journey.

☁️

Remote Management

Open counters, reroute queues, push announcements, and update branding from anywhere — your phone, laptop, or tablet.

Visitor Journey

How Kiosk Works

A simple, three-step experience that delights every visitor.

🎫

1. Get Token

Walk up to the kiosk, scan a QR code, or use WhatsApp to get a digital token with your estimated wait time.

📱

2. Wait Freely

Shop, work, or relax. You get an SMS alert when your turn is just a few ahead — so your time is never wasted.

✅

3. Get Served

Your token number appears on the display board with counter assignment. Walk in, get served, leave a quick rating.
