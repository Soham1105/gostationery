Features

One machine. Everything your team needs.

From document printing to stationery dispensing — fully automated, fully connected, zero friction.

🖨️

Self-Service Document Printing

Upload PDFs, Word docs, or images via the touchscreen, USB, or send from email/WhatsApp. Print in B&W or colour — no IT setup required.

📦

Stationery Dispensing

Dispense pens, papers, files, stamps, and more from a customisable tray layout. Set prices per item — the machine handles the rest.

💳

Cashless Payments

Accept UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), debit/credit cards, and digital wallets. Automated billing and invoice generation built in.

☁️

Remote Monitoring

View machine status, transaction history, stock levels, and revenue from any device — your cloud dashboard, always live.

🔔

Automated Stock Alerts

Get instant SMS and dashboard notifications when any item runs low — never let the machine run out of paper or supplies.

📊

Usage Analytics

Track which items are most popular, peak usage hours, revenue per machine, and stock turnover — all in exportable reports.

🎨

Custom Branding

The machine enclosure, screen interface, and printed receipts can all be branded with your logo and colours — looks like your own product.

🔒

Access Control

Restrict usage by employee ID card, QR code, or PIN. Perfect for corporate offices, universities, and government departments.

🔧

OTA Software Updates

New features, pricing changes, and UI updates are pushed remotely — no technician visits, no downtime, always up to date.

User Journey

How the Station Works

Three taps and you're done. No training required.

📱

1. Choose & Upload

Select document printing or stationery on the touchscreen. Upload your file or pick items from the menu.

💳

2. Pay Cashlessly

Scan UPI QR, tap your card, or use any digital wallet. Payment confirmed instantly — receipt on screen or WhatsApp.

✅

3. Collect

Your printout or stationery item is dispensed within seconds. Done — no waiting, no staff, no queues.
